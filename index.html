What is UNO?
Uno is a popular card game that can be played with 2 to 10 players. The objective of the game is to be the first player to get rid of all your cards. The game is played with a special deck of cards consisting of four colors (red, blue, green, and yellow), each color with cards numbered from 0 to 9, plus action cards such as Skip, Reverse, Draw Two, and Wild cards. The rules of the game are relatively simple, and it can be enjoyed by players of all ages.
